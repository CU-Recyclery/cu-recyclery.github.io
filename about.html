Our Mission

The goal of the Clemson Recyclery is to distribute bikes to those in the Clemson community who need them the most and along the way educating others on repairing, maintaining, and riding bikes. And of course having fun!

History

Draft paragraph of what text will be on this page. Starting in YEAR the Clemson Recyclery was created to help repair and distribute used bikes to Clemson University students encouraging sustainability and bike safety. Unfortunately, due to Covid-19 the organization was forced to close it's doors.

Finding the old facebook page and noticing a lack of bike awareness on campus, students restarted the organization in 2023. We now meet at the Andy Quattlebaum Center for Outdoor Activities

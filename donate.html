Donate to the Clemson Recyclery

Donations helps keep the Recyclery running strong and encourages a more sustainable Clemson community! We take your old bikes and fix them up for others to use and enjoy. If you don't have a bike, but would like to support our mission you can donate funds.

Donation Guidelines

Bikes

We accept bikes of any condition whether ready to ride or has seen better days. Recycling parts from bikes that are beyond saving helps us keep our costs down and saves the earth piece by piece! Anything that we can't use we will properly recycle or dispose of.

Parts

We accept a ride variety of parts to be donated as well! Brakes, tires, chains, and gears are all great candidates for reuse. Damaged frames, spokes, and old helmets will be properly disposed of as they are inadequate to be reused.

Accessories/Apparel

Need to clean out your closet and bike junk drawer as well? We accept lights, waterbottles, clothing, fenders, commuter bags and more! Damaged or very old helmets however will be disposed of due to safety concerns.
